Everyone Loves Norm

A Chilling Tale of a Man with No Emotion... and No Mercy

About the Book

Norm is quiet. Norm is polite. Norm never smiles — not even when he's killing.

Everyone Loves Norm is a psychological horror novel that follows the life of a seemingly normal man who harbors a dark, violent secret. With a face as cold as stone and a heart colder still, Norm moves silently through a city that never suspects the truth.

“He didn’t scream. He didn’t smile. He just stared — and then he ended them.”

What Makes Norm Different?

Unlike your typical villain, Norm doesn't act out of rage. He isn't driven by revenge. There’s no motive. No emotion. Just the silence of death and the straight face that delivers it.

Can You Handle the Truth?

Step into the shadows of Norm’s mind — if you dare. Discover why everyone *thinks* they love Norm… until it's too late.
